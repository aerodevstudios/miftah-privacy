Miftah Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 22, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy explains how Miftah handles data in the Android app.

Miftah helps users pause before opening selected distracting apps by showing a short dhikr flow, a daily streak screen, an ayah for the session, and an unlock screen.

Miftah is designed to keep protected app choices and devotional activity local on the user's device.

Data Miftah Stores Locally

Miftah may store the following data on your device:

  • App settings, such as language, dhikr count, pause length, enabled dhikr types, and whether ayah or streak screens are enabled.
  • Protected app choices.
  • Local unlock session state, including when the current unlock window expires.
  • Streak state and lightweight pause counters.
  • Last shown ayah identifier, used to avoid immediate repetition.

This data is stored locally on your device and is not sold.

Installed Apps And Protected Apps

Miftah may show a list of launchable apps installed on your device so you can choose which apps to protect.

Your selected protected apps and app identifiers are stored locally on your device. Miftah does not send your protected app names, package IDs, installed app list, or raw app usage history to ads, analytics, or external servers.

Android Accessibility Service

Miftah uses Android Accessibility only for its app protection feature.

When you enable the Miftah Accessibility service, Miftah can detect when one of your selected protected apps opens. If your unlock session has expired, Miftah opens the dhikr pause flow before the protected app unlocks.

Miftah does not use Accessibility to read:

  • Messages.
  • Passwords.
  • Keystrokes.
  • Form fields.
  • Screen text.
  • Personal conversations.

The Accessibility service is used only for the narrow purpose of detecting selected app openings and starting the Miftah pause flow.

Quran, Dhikr, And Dua Content

Quran ayat, dhikr, and duas are stored locally in the app. Miftah does not send your devotional content, viewed ayat, dhikr activity, or duas to ad networks or analytics services.

Sources:

  • Arabic Quran text: Tanzil Project.
  • English translation: Rowwad Translation Center via QuranEnc/quran-api.

Advertising

Miftah is free and supported by ads.

Ads may be served by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may collect or use data such as IP address, device identifiers, ad interactions, diagnostics, and other ad serving signals according to Google's policies.

Miftah does not send your protected app choices, installed app list, app usage history, dhikr content, ayah content, or duas to AdMob.

Google's explanation of how it uses data from partner apps is available here: Google partner sites policy.

Google Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure: Google Mobile Ads SDK data disclosure.

Analytics

Miftah does not currently use a separate analytics SDK.

If analytics are added in the future, this policy will be updated before release of that version.

Data Sharing

Miftah does not sell personal data.

Miftah does not share protected app choices, installed app lists, package IDs, raw app usage history, dhikr content, ayah content, or duas with third parties.

AdMob may process advertising-related data as described in Google's policies.

Data Security

Local app data is stored on your device using app storage. Advertising data handled by Google AdMob is handled according to Google's SDK and service policies, including encryption in transit where described by Google.

Children's Privacy

Miftah is not designed specifically for children under 13. If the target audience changes in a future release, this policy and the app's ads setup will be reviewed for child-directed requirements.

Changes To This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated when app features, data handling, ads, or third-party services change.
